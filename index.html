About Me

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. My research has been featured in Operations Research, IEEE Transactions, Computers in Industry, and the International Journal of Production Research.
I obtained his Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from The Pennsylvania State University – University Park. My research interests is in stochastic modeling and analytics for smart manufacturing and service systems.
